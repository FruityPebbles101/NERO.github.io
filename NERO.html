Who is NERO?

NERO is a British electronic music group. Daniel “Dan” Stephens, Joseph “Joe” Ray, and Alana Watson are the members of NERO.
The genres NERO overall makes is dubstep, drum and bass, electronica, and future house.
This wide variety of music genre incorporated in their songs made them popular in the early 2000s.
